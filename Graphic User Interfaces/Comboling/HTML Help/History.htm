History

 

The game Comboling was conceptualized by Jerome Lulling while he was looking at the tiles on his bathroom floor.  If you would like to know more about Jerome Lulling then visit his website at www.JeromeLulling.com or his contact and information page at http://webplaza.pt.lu/public/jlulling/contact.htm

 

As of now the game Comboling exists as a game online only.  I wanted to bring it out of it’s shell and turn it into a windows application just to see how tricky some parts of the game could be.  To my disadvantage I went in not knowing about how Jerome figured out how to create a field that was always solvable.  The time that figuring this out has taken me left the game a tad on the incomplete side.  Hopefully I will be able to add in the time element this Christmas break but I am confident that I have still created an entertaining game for everybody interested in a challenging puzzle.  Please contact me if you confront an unsolvable puzzle.  Take a screenshot and email it to me: vanilla.frijole@gmail.com
